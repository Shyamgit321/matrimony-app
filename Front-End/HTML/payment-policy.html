Payment Policy

This page explains the payment terms, subscription rules, and billing conditions for MySoul services.

1. Payment Methods

We accept UPI, Net Banking, Debit/Credit Cards, and Wallet payments through secure third-party gateways.

2. Subscription Services

Premium features like profile boosting, messaging, and visibility enhancements are available through paid plans.

All subscriptions are for the selected duration only and are non-transferable.

3. Pricing & Taxes

All prices are inclusive of applicable taxes unless specified. Prices may change based on promotional offers.

4. Refund Policy

  • Payments are non-refundable once services are activated.
  • No refund for user inactivity, match results, or user dissatisfaction.
  • Refunds are allowed only in case of duplicate payments or technical errors.

5. Failed Transactions

If an amount is deducted but service is not activated, it will be auto-refunded within 3–7 business days.

6. Auto-Renewal

MySoul currently does not use auto-renewal. Users must manually purchase plans when needed.

7. Fraudulent Activity

Any misuse of payment methods or chargeback abuse may result in account suspension.

8. Contact Support

Email: support@mysoul.com

Support: 24–48 hours response time
