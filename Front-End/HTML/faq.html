Online Help - FAQ

Safety & Security

How do I report suspicious activity?
Go to the profile → Tap “Report User” → Choose reason.
Is my personal data safe?
We use encrypted storage and secure authentication to protect your data.

Profile Help

How do I update my profile?
Go to Menu → Edit Profile → Save changes.
Why is my photo not approved?
Photos must be clear, recent, and show your face properly.

Account Issues

How do I reset my password?
Use the “Forgot Password” option → enter email → reset link sent.
How can I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

Matches & Connections

How are matches suggested?
Matches are based on preferences, compatibility, and location.
Why am I not receiving matches?
Update your preferences such as age, caste, or location to get better results.
