MySoul Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MySoul collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your name, email, phone number, preferences, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve user experience & recommendations
  • Security & fraud prevention
  • App performance improvement

3. Sharing of Information

Your data is shared only with verified partners or legal authorities.

4. Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and authentication layers to keep your data safe.

5. User Rights

You can edit or delete your data anytime.

6. Policy Updates

We may update our policy and notify users for major changes.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, analytics tools, and tracking technologies for security and personalization.

8. Data Retention

Your data is stored only as long as necessary for service and legal reasons.

9. Children’s Privacy

MySoul is strictly for users 18+. We do not collect data from minors.

10. Third-Party Services

We use third-party partners for hosting, analytics, and communication services.

11. Account Deletion

You may request full account deletion from settings or by contacting support.

12. Contact Information
